PhoneGap Kickstarter

This Cordova Template lets you start your app really fast. You can just begin developing your web app with jQuery Mobile without worries.

It's tested and pre-configured to create your app for various devices with PhoneGap Build.

Zip the folder and upload it in your https://build.phonegap.com acoount.

ReplaceIcons and Splash screens with your custom designs.

Edit index.html and app.js for your custom JavaScript application.
